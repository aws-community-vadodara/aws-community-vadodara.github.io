WHO ARE WE?

AWS User Group Vadodara is a Vadodara based group united by community-led learnings and diversified by the wide variety of tools and technologies closely knit with Amazon Web Services, cloud computing technology, AWS design implementation and servicing, high scalability / performance computing, production use cases of AWS, blockchain, Artificial Intelligence and much more.

AWS Community Days are community-organized cloud education events, featuring technical discussions and demos led by expert AWS users and industry leaders from around the world

Community-based learning is the best way to enhance the concepts being taught by connecting with enthusiasts in person, thereby sharing first-hand experiences and familiar, accessible examples.

All skills levels are welcome to help accomplish our motto of learning, sharing and networking. Let's join hands to empower this tech community to make it bigger and better!

         

What to expect from AWS AI/ML Community Day?

  • Welcome to the AI ML One Day Event!
  • Join us for an immersive and informative one-day event dedicated to Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML).
  • This event brings together industry experts, and enthusiasts to explore the latest advancements, trends, and applications in the exciting field of AI and ML.
  • Discover the power of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) with AWS AI/ML Day, a comprehensive one-day event designed to educate and empower attendees on AWS's cutting-edge AI and ML technologies.
  • Join us for a day of insightful presentations, discussion, demonstration, and networking opportunities with industry experts and fellow AI/ML enthusiasts.
  • Harness the power of networking with 250+ AWS users, MLOps, Data Analytics experts, Generative AI experts and more.
